Redefining Hand Stability

G.R.A.S.P. utilizes advanced sensor technology and precision servo motors to counteract hand tremors, returning control to those affected by Parkinson's disease.

Discover G.R.A.S.P.

Designed for Real Life

G.R.A.S.P. is more than a medical device — it's a pathway to restored independence and confidence.

Accessible Materials

Built with cardboard, foam, plastic, and some metal parts to keep it lightweight and easy to use for everyone. Our focus was on creating an affordable prototype that demonstrates the core concept.

Basic Control System

Our current prototype uses a control panel that receives input and coordinates with batteries to deliver the right amount of power to servo motors, helping stabilize finger movements.

Parkinson's Assistance

Designed to help with hand tremors from Parkinson's disease, our prototype demonstrates how simple technology can enhance grip stability and hand control.

Precision Engineering

Every component of G.R.A.S.P. has been meticulously designed to work in perfect harmony.
